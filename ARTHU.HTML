ARTHUNA TEMPLE

*PLACE OF MAHI MAHOTSAV*

THE OLD NAME OF THE PLACE WAS UTTHUNAKA.IT WAS THE CAPITAL OF
PARAMARA RULERS OF VAGAD DURING ELEVENTH-TWELVE CENTURIES A.D.
THEY PATRONIZED BOTH JAINISM AND SAIVISM SIMULTANEOUSLY,SO THEY
CONSTRUCT A NUMBER OF SHIVA TEMPLES
