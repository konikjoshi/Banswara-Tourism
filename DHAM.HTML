MAANGARH DHAM

*BANSWARA'S JALLIANWALA BAGH*

GOVIND GURU,WAS A POPULAR SANT OF THE BHILS WHO MADE THE COMMUNITY AND
FILLED THEM WITH A SENSE OF PATRIOTISM.THE BHILLS WERE SO INSPIRED THAT
SACRIFICED THEIR LIVES WHILE FIGHTING AGAINST THE BRITISH ARMY.IT IS
THEREFORE ALSO KNOWN AS "JALLIANWALA BAGH OF BANSWARA".
