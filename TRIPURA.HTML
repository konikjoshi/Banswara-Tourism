TRIPURA SUNDARI

*THE PROUD OF BANSWARA*

THERE IS A TEMPLE OF GODDESS TRIPURA SUNDARI KNOWN AS TARTAI MATA HERE,WHICHHAS
BEAUTIFULL IDOL OF BLACK STONE HAVING 18 HANDS EACH CARRYING A SYMBOL,WHILE THE
GODDESS IS SEEN RIDING A TIGER.THE EXACT DATE OF CONSTRUCTION OF THIS TEMPLE IS
NOT KNOWN,IT IS SAID TO HAVE BEEN BUILT BEFORE SAMRAT KANISHKA,WHO RULED HERE.
IT IS ONE OF THR'SHAKTI PEETHS'OF THE HINDUS.
