MADARESHWAR

*TEMPLE OF LORD SHIVA*


THIS IS A FAMOUS TEMPLE OF LORD SHIVA BUILT INSIDE A
NUTURAL CAVE ON TOP OF A HILL TOWARD THE EASTERN PART
OF THE CITY.IT PROVIDES A SENSATIONAL VIEW.IN THE SEASON
OF SHIVA(SHIVRATRI)MOST OF PEOPLE CAME TO THE CITY AS THE
NUMBER OF 2 LAKHS AND CLIMB UP UP-TO THE TOP OF HILL AND
PRAY FOR ITSELF AND HER OWN FAMILY MEMBER.IT IS VERY NATURAL
CAVE NOT INDUSTRIAL PIECE.
